Heather and her husband, Evan, have been married for nearly 10 years, after a mutually unflattering introduction and through many encounters. They met in Cleveland, where they still live, around the corner from their best friends, who actually served to bring them together. Heather and Evan have twin three-year-old boys, Harold & Clive, who keep life entertaining and exhausting.
The daughter of two teachers and a lifelong learner herself, Heather has both a bachelor's and master's degree in music performance, while also completing a certificate of Biblical Counseling from the Southeastern Baptist Theological Seminary, done some certificate courses through the Christian Counseling and Education Foundation, and is now completing a course in full-stack web development through Coding Temple.
Heather was a barista, supervisor and manager for Starbucks, her time as a partner lasting nearly 15 years, and has been now in church administration for 6 years. Aside from learning, Heather has dabbled in writing music reviews and loves baking, reading, puzzles, and any manner of soft textiles and beverages of all kinds.
