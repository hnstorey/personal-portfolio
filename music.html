Music

Heather began playing oboe in 6th grade after initially starting on flute.
Even after numerous warnings of the difficulty of the instrument, Heather was undaunted.
Her double-reed playing has taken her on tours of Europe and China, as well as several cities in America.
She's been a member of the Erie Philharmonic in Erie, PA since 2008 where she loves being a supportive second oboist
and being part of a larger whole.
